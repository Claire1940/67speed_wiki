## Optimizing Your Rebirth Strategy

The final hurdle in any tycoon challenge is knowing when to rebirth. While the **67 game tracker** shows your current earnings, it also calculates the potential bonus of a rebirth. Rebirthing resets your progress but provides a permanent multiplier to your luck or earnings. 

In a timed challenge, such as the 1-hour trillion-dollar sprint, a rebirth can be a double-edged sword. If you rebirth too early, you lose the momentum of your current high-EPS items. If you rebirth too late, you miss out on the 2x or 4x luck bonuses that make trillion-dollar items possible. Most expert players suggest using the **67 game tracker** to identify the "plateau point"—the moment where your EPS growth slows down significantly despite constant upgrades.

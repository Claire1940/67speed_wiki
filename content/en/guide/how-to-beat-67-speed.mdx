## Strategies for How to Beat 67 Speed

To truly understand **how to beat 67 speed**, you must experiment with your physical positioning. Unlike traditional games where you sit still, this is a full-body experience. ### 1. The Proximity Advantage
One of the most effective ways to increase your score is to stand as close to the camera as possible without losing the tracking of your hands. When you are close to the lens, a small movement of your arm covers a larger percentage of the screen's pixels. This translates to higher velocity readings in the game's engine.

### 2. The Wide Motion Technique
While it is tempting to make small, vibrating movements with your hands, the game often rewards wider, more deliberate arcs. By moving your arms fully up and down, you create a clear path for the motion tracker to follow.

> **💡 Tip:** If you feel your hands getting tired, switch from "vibration" style movements to "wide-arc" movements. This engages larger muscle groups like your shoulders and back, allowing for longer sessions.

### 3. Maintaining Momentum
The 67 Speed leaderboard is dominated by those who can maintain a consistent rhythm. Stopping even for a second to catch your breath can cause your multiplier to reset or your average speed to plummet.

**Q: Why is my score not counting even when I move fast?**

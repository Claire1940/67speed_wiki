## Strategies for Maximum Speed

Achieving a high score on **67speed.clm** isn't just about clicking as fast as possible; it's about efficiency of motion. Many players make the mistake of using large, sweeping arm movements, which increases the time between inputs. To master the 67 speed challenge, follow these tactical steps:

1.  **Minimize Travel Distance:** Keep your hand as close to the trigger point as possible. Smaller movements result in faster cycles.
2.  **The "Jitter" Technique:** Rather than clicking with your finger muscles alone, vibrate your entire forearm to generate high-frequency clicks.
3.  **Hand Switching:** If the challenge allows for multi-hand input, practice switching hands to give your dominant hand a brief rest during longer sessions.
4.  **Rhythm Breathing:** Don't hold your breath. Maintaining a steady oxygen flow helps prevent muscle fatigue during the final seconds of the test.

> **💡 Tip:** Ensure your hands are clean and dry. As noted by many streamers, "well water" or minerals on the hands can actually affect your grip and mouse glide, leading to sub-optimal performance.

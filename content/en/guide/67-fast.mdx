## Advanced Techniques for 67 Fast Success

To move beyond the average scores, you must adopt the "Double-Tap" or "Butterfly" methods used by professionals. These techniques allow for multiple inputs in the same timeframe it takes a standard player to make one.

### The Jitter Method
This involves tensing the arm and wrist muscles to the point of vibration. While difficult to master, it provides the most consistent high-speed input. Experts suggest keeping the wrist slightly elevated from the desk to allow for a greater range of motion.

### The Butterfly Technique
Commonly used in clicking games, this can be adapted for the **67 fast** challenge by using two fingers on the primary input keys. By alternating fingers (index and middle), you effectively double the potential input frequency.

### Rhythm Training
Speed is nothing without accuracy. If a player inputs "66" or "77" instead of "67," the sequence is often invalidated or penalized. Using a metronome set to high BPM (Beats Per Minute) can help in developing the internal clock necessary to maintain a steady flow for the full 20 seconds.

## Training Routine for Aspiring Champions

If you want to improve your performance, follow this structured training routine designed by the 67 Wiki Team. Consistency is more important than the total number of hours spent in a single day.

*   **Warm-up (5 Minutes):** Slow, rhythmic typing or clicking at 50% of your max speed to wake up the muscles.
*   **Burst Intervals (10 Minutes):** Perform 5-second sprints followed by 10-second rests. This builds explosive speed.

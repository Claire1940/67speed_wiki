## Infiltration Strategies for Key Locations

Success in the **67 world** often depends on your ability to use "Extreme Camouflage." Standard stealth is rarely enough to bypass the advanced AI of the 2026 NPCs. Players must utilize environmental disguises to reach the inner sanctums where brain rots are stored.

### Foltyn's House: The Electrician Gambit
To secure the Ballerina Cappuccina, players must bypass Foltyn's streaming setup. The most effective method is the "Electrician Disguise." By posing as a utility worker, you can gain entry under the guise of fixing a Wi-Fi outage. While inside, use decoys—such as a cardboard dummy or a fake pizza delivery—to distract the resident while you search the upper floors.

### Ko's House: The Punching Bag Strategy
The rare 67 Brain Rot is located within a safe in Ko's residence. The 2026 safe combination is **6767**. To enter the house undetected, players can camouflage themselves as a heavy punching bag. NPCs like the "World's Strongest Man" (Pebble) will inadvertently carry you into the training area, providing a direct route to the safe.

### The Police Station Vault
The police station represents one of the highest difficulty spikes in **67 world**. To access the vault, you must hide within a bin of "stolen money." 

1. **Equip the Money Bin Camouflage.**
2. **Wait for a "Money Delivery" event.**
3. **Once inside the vault, you have 10 minutes of oxygen.**
4. **Locate the cell keys on the wall to facilitate a quick exit.**

## High-Tier Item Locations and Requirements

As you progress deeper into the **67 world**, the items become increasingly difficult to obtain, often requiring specific "prank" triggers or secret room activations.

### Zong's Secret Rainbow Room
Zong's house is guarded by high-level security, but the Dragon Candalone is worth the risk. To find the secret room, you must navigate to the bathroom area.
*   **The Shower Trigger:** Activating the shower reveals a hidden passage.
*   **Dance Activation:** The final door is camera-activated. You must perform a specific emote to unlock the vault.
*   **Decoy Usage:** Use a high-value distraction, such as a fake celebrity appearance (e.g., a Ronaldo lookalike), to draw Zong out of the house.

### The Forest Bunker and the King Cultist
The rarest item in the game, the Golden Strawberry Elephant, is held by the King Cultist in the deep forest. This area is populated by hostile cultists who can be neutralized using blow darts or bear traps.

| Step | Action | Objective |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Navigate to "6 Trees Down, 7 Left" | Locate the Bunker Entrance |
| **2** | Use the "Pizza Box" Decoy | Distract the Bunker Guards |
| **3** | Obtain the Autograph | Neutralize the King Cultist peacefully |
| **4** | Loot the Gold Chest | Secure the **Golden Strawberry Elephant** |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to fight the King Cultist directly. His HP pool is scaled to be unbeatable in 2026. Negotiation via the "Fan Interaction" mechanic is the only 100% viable path.

## 67 World: Aura Management

Your Aura level is a dynamic stat that fluctuates based on your actions. Stealing items without getting caught increases your "Street Aura," while being caught or using "negative" items (like the "Worst Brain Rot") will significantly penalize your standing.

**Positive Aura Actions:**
*   Successfully using a camouflage disguise.
*   Solving safe puzzles on the first attempt.
*   Rescuing kidnapped NPCs like 67 or the Dino Kid.

**Negative Aura Actions:**
*   Getting caught trespassing by Officer Young Blood.
*   Selling rare brain rots to low-level vendors.
*   Failing a "Dance Activation" sequence.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to gain aura in 67 world?**

A: The most efficient way to build aura is to complete the "Forest Bounty" questline. Successfully infiltrating the King Cultist's bunker and securing the Golden Strawberry Elephant provides a massive permanent boost to your stats.

**Q: Where can I find the safe code for Ko's house?**

A: The safe code is **6767**. It is a reference to the player 67's favorite number and is consistent across all 2026 server instances.

**Q: How do I escape the Police Vault before the oxygen runs out?**

A: You must locate the cell keys on the wall opposite the money bins. Once you have the keys, you can exit through the side ventilation shaft, which bypasses the main vault door.

**Q: Is "Tongue Tongue" still in the game?**

A: No, "Tongue Tongue" was officially removed from the game in the early 2026 update. Players often post "RIP Tongue Tongue" in the community forums to commemorate the item. Finding a "Tongue Tongue Sor" item is now considered a legacy achievement.

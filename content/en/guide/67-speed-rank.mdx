## Troubleshooting Common Mistakes

Many players get stuck just below the **67 speed rank** because of small, repetitive errors. One of the most common issues is "Input Overlap," where a player tries to initiate a jump before the previous dash animation has cleared its cooldown. This results in a "Dead Input," causing the character to stand still for a fraction of a second.

Another frequent pitfall is "Greed Deaths." While it is tempting to go for the most difficult skips to save time, a single death will instantly invalidate a **67 speed rank** run. It is often better to take a "Safety Route" that is 2 seconds slower than to risk a 30-second reset due to a failed clip.

1.  **Record your gameplay:** Use software to analyze your runs frame-by-frame.
2.  **Focus on one map at a time:** Don't try to master the entire game at once. Get a "Gold" time on every map before pushing for the **67 speed rank**.
3.  **Join the Discord community:** Other runners often share "Seed-Specific" tips that can help you deal with RNG (Random Number Generation) in certain levels.

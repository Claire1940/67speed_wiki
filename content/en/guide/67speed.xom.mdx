## Navigating House Beneviento
House Beneviento is widely considered the psychological peak of Resident Evil Village. Unlike the combat-heavy sections of Castle Dimitrescu, this area strips Ethan Winters of his weapons, forcing a reliance on pure puzzle-solving and stealth. ### The Mannequin Puzzle Steps
Upon entering the workshop, you are presented with a mannequin that resembles Mia. Follow these steps to clear the room in record time:

1.  **Examine the Right Arm:** Remove the piece to reveal the winding key.
2.  **Examine the Left Leg:** Remove the knee joint to find the sliver key.
3.  **The Ring:** Take the blood-covered ring from the left hand and wash it in the nearby sink to reveal the door code (**05-29-11**).
4.  **The Music Box:** Use the winding key on the music box. You must align the cylinders based on the scratches on the wood. 

| Cylinder Position | Visual Cue |

| **Cylinder 1** | Deepest scratch on the left edge |

| **Cylinder 3** | Large notch in the center |
| **Cylinder 4** | Matching notch for the right side |
| **Cylinder 5** | Clean edge on the far right |

## Surviving the "Baby" Encounter
The most infamous part of the Beneviento basement is the encounter with the gargantuan, mutated fetus. This monster is unkillable, and contact results in an immediate game over. In a **67speed.xom** run, your goal is to trigger the elevator power as fast as possible without getting cornered.

### Stealth and Evasion Tactics
*   **The Bed Strategy:** When the baby blocks the hallway leading back to the elevator, retreat to the bedroom. Hide under the bed only as a last resort. It is faster to kite the monster around the center table if your movement speed is high enough.
*   **The Fuse Box:** Once you obtain the fuse from the kitchen area, you must loop back through the study. The baby will spawn in the main corridor; use the side rooms to break its line of sight.
*   **The Elevator:** Don't wait for the doors to open fully. Mash the interact button as you approach to shave off precious seconds.

## Boss Fight: Donna and Angie
The fight against Donna Beneviento is a game of hide-and-seek with her doll, Angie. To win quickly, you must find Angie three times before the other dolls overwhelm you.

1.  **First Location:** Usually upstairs in the side room.
2.  **Second Location:** Often on the floor in the kitchen or near the sofa in the living area.
3.  **Third Location:** Near the elevator entrance or the front door.

> **💡 Tip:** Angie’s location is signaled by a distinct giggling sound. Use high-quality spatial audio or a 2026 headset to pinpoint her location without having to visually scan every corner.

## FAQ

**Q: What exactly is the 67speed.xom challenge?**

A: It is a community-defined playstyle for Resident Evil Village that focuses on high-intensity movement and rapid-fire puzzle completion. It is often associated with streamers who push their physical and mental limits to achieve fast completion times.

**Q: How do I open the door in the House Beneviento doll room?**

A: The code is **05-29-11**. This can be found on the inside of the wedding ring after washing the blood off it in the sink located in the adjacent room.

**Q: Why do I keep getting caught by the baby monster?**

A: The baby monster reacts to sound and proximity. Avoid running when you are in the same room as the monster. Use the beds and lockers as hiding spots, but remember that kiting the monster around large furniture is often a faster strategy for advanced players.

**Q: Is there any way to kill the dolls in the final House Beneviento fight?**

A: No, the dolls cannot be killed with traditional weapons. You must find the Angie doll three times and interact with her using the scissors to end the hallucination and defeat Donna Beneviento.

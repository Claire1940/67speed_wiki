## Physical Clicking Techniques for сайт 67 speed

To maximize your score on the **сайт 67 speed** leaderboard, you must move beyond standard index-finger clicking. The community has developed several specialized methods to bypass the physical limitations of human reaction time.

### Jitter Clicking
Jitter clicking involves tensing the muscles in your arm and wrist until they begin to vibrate or "jitter." This vibration is transferred to the finger, resulting in extremely high-frequency clicks. While effective, it can be physically taxing and requires significant practice to maintain accuracy.

### Butterfly Clicking
This technique uses two fingers (usually the index and middle) to alternate hits on the mouse button or keys. By timing the strikes so that one finger rises while the other falls, you can effectively double your input rate compared to single-finger clicking.

### Drag Clicking
Drag clicking is the most controversial and difficult technique. By sliding a finger across the surface of a mouse with enough friction, the switch is triggered dozens of times in a single motion. This is often used to reach astronomical CPS numbers, though some **сайт 67 speed** tournaments may have specific rules regarding its use.

## Hardware and Software Optimization

Your hardware can be a significant bottleneck when trying to achieve a high score on **сайт 67 speed**. If your mouse has a high "debounce time," it may ignore rapid successive clicks to prevent accidental double-clicking. For this game, you want the lowest debounce time possible.

1.  **High-Polling Rate Mice:** Ensure your mouse is set to 1000Hz or 8000Hz polling rates to reduce the delay between a physical click and the computer registering the input.
2.  **Mechanical Keyboards:** If using a keyboard for inputs, silver or "speed" switches with short actuation points are preferred in 2026.
3.  **Browser Performance:** Use a lightweight browser with hardware acceleration enabled. Close all background tabs to ensure the JavaScript timer on the site runs with millisecond precision.
4.  **Monitor Refresh Rate:** While the game isn't graphically intensive, a 240Hz or 360Hz monitor helps you track the real-time counter more smoothly, providing better visual feedback for your rhythm.

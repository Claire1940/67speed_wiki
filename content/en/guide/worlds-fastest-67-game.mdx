## Mastering the Worlds Fastest 67 Game: Advanced Strategies

Achieving a score north of 500 requires more than just fast fingers; it requires a specialized approach to input methodology. There are three primary techniques used by the top 100 players in the world.

### Jitter Clicking
This involves vibrating the muscles in your forearm to create a rapid-fire clicking motion. While highly effective for short bursts, maintaining jitter clicking for the full 20 seconds of the **worlds fastest 67 game** can be physically taxing.

### Butterfly Clicking
By using two fingers to alternate clicks on a single button (or key), players can effectively double their input frequency. This is generally considered the most sustainable method for breaking the 560-point barrier.

### Drag Clicking
Utilizing the friction between the skin and the mouse surface to register multiple clicks in a single downward motion. While this offers the highest theoretical CPS, it is often the hardest to control when trying to specifically output the number 67.

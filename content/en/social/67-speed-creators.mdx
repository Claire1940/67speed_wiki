## Optimizing Track Performance

A common pitfall for many **67 speed creators** is neglecting optimization. High-fidelity assets can lead to frame drops, which are lethal in a game focused on split-second reactions. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Excessive use of dynamic lighting in high-speed sections can cause "strobe lag," leading to dropped inputs on mid-range hardware.

To optimize your levels:
*   **Occlusion Culling:** Ensure that objects not in the player's line of sight are not being rendered.
*   **LOD (Level of Detail) Scaling:** Use lower-resolution models for distant background scenery.
*   **Trigger Zoning:** Only activate complex logic scripts when the player enters a specific radius.

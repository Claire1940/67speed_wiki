## Strategies for Breaking the 67 Barrier

To move beyond the limitations tracked by your performance data, you must optimize your physical environment. High-speed gaming in 2026 relies heavily on minimizing external distractions and technical bottlenecks.

### Device Optimization
Input lag is the silent killer of high-score runs. Ensure your device is in "Game Mode" and that background applications are closed. A **67 speed tracker** will often show that "misses" at high levels are actually the result of the screen not registering a touch in time due to CPU throttling.

### Finger Placement and Pressure
Many players tap too hard when they get nervous. This increases the "release time" of your finger, adding milliseconds to your response. 
* Use a light, glancing tap.
* Keep your finger as close to the screen as possible without touching it.
* Focus on the leading edge of the block, not the center.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid playing while your device is charging if it tends to overheat, as thermal throttling can cause sudden frame drops exactly when the speed picks up.

## Master the Rebirth System

The most critical mechanic for long-term success in **67 speed clicker** is the Rebirth system. Many players make the mistake of hoardings trillions of 67s without ever rebirthing. When you rebirth, your current 67 currency resets to zero, but you keep all your pets and gain a permanent multiplier to all future earnings.

### Why Rebirthing is Essential:
*   **Keep Your Pets**: You do not lose your high-tier Ronaldo or Pet 54 units.
*   **Exponential Growth**: Each rebirth makes your next billion much easier to reach.
*   **Trillionaire Status**: Reaching 15 to 20 rebirths is the only realistic way to earn 78 trillion 67s per click.

If you find yourself stuck at a specific monetary value, it is a sign that you have waited too long to rebirth. A good rule of thumb in 2026 is to rebirth as soon as the cost of the next area feels "impossible" or takes more than 10 minutes of active clicking to reach.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always double-check that your best pets are equipped before your first click after a rebirth. This ensures you jump immediately back into the millions within seconds.
